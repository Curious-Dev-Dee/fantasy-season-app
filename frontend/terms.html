4 Game Rules & Fair Play

Cricket Experts is a season-long fantasy cricket game. Key rules include:

  • Each user selects 11 players within a 100 credit budget per match, subject to role minimums and overseas caps as defined in the app.
  • Teams lock automatically at the actual match start time. No changes are accepted after the lock.
  • Fantasy points are calculated from real match data processed by the platform admin. Point calculations are final once published.
  • Boosters are one-use-each, applicable only during the designated league stage window. Misuse or exploitation of booster bugs must be reported and will be reversed.
  • The platform operates on a substitution budget system. Circumventing sub counting or exploiting system errors is prohibited.
  • Prediction results, All Stars XI scores and Daily XI scores are calculated automatically and are final.

No real money is involved. Cricket Experts is a free-to-play entertainment platform. There are no entry fees, no prize money and no gambling elements of any kind.
