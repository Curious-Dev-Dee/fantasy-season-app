How to Play

Squad Building

Pick your 11-player squad with a 100 Credit budget:

  • WK: 1 (Wicketkeeper)
  • BAT: 3 - 6 (Batsmen)
  • AR: 1 - 4 (All-Rounders)
  • BOWL: 3 - 6 (Bowlers)
  • Overseas: Max 4

Transfer Strategy

Manage your subs carefully across the season:

League Stage (Matches 2-70)130 Subs
Playoffs (Matches 72-74)10 Subs

⚡ RESET WINDOWS: Matches #1 (Season Start) and #71 (Playoff Start) allow Unlimited Free Subs.

*1 Free uncapped per match & Earn +1 Free Sub for every 10 correct match predictions in the Fun Zone!

Match Boosters

Use these 7 unique power-ups once each during the League Stage (Match 2-70):

TOTAL 2X 2x points for your whole team.
INDIAN 2X 2x points for all Indian players.
CAPTAIN 3X Triple (3x) points for Captain.
OVERSEAS 2X 2x points for Overseas players.
UNCAPPED 2X 2x points for Uncapped players.
FREE 11 Unlimited transfers for 1 match.
MOM 2X 2x points for Man of the Match.

*One booster can be used per match. Each booster can only be used once.

What are Fun Zone Stars?
For every correct match prediction, you earn 1 Star. Collect 10 Stars to automatically receive 1 Free Substitution added to your account!
How does "Free 11" work?
When you activate the Free 11 booster, any substitutions you make for that specific match will cost 0 subs. Perfect for heavy rotation!
When do teams lock?
Teams lock exactly at the match start time. If the match is delayed, the timer updates automatically.
