Can I change my team name after signing up?
No. Your team name and full name are locked permanently once you set them during your first sign-in. Choose wisely!
What happens if I run out of subs?
You won't be able to save a team that would cost more subs than you have remaining. The Save button will show "NOT ENOUGH SUBS". You can still change your Captain/VC without using subs.
Does changing C/VC cost a sub?
No. You can change your Captain and Vice-Captain as many times as you want without spending any subs. Only swapping a player in or out of your XI costs subs.
What is the free uncapped swap?
Each match, one uncapped player swap is free — it doesn't count toward your subs. An uncapped player is an Indian domestic player who hasn't represented India. Look for the 🧢 badge in the player pool.
What if a match is abandoned or rained off?
If a match is abandoned before a ball is bowled, any subs you used for that match are refunded, your booster is refunded, and the match doesn't count for fantasy. It's like it never happened.

If a match is delayed (rain break, then resumes), the countdown updates to the new start time and you can keep editing your team until then.
When do I get my subs reset for playoffs?
Match 81 is a free unlimited reset — you can change your entire XI without using any subs. After Match 81 locks, you start the playoff stage with a fresh budget of 10 subs for Matches 82–84.
How does the Free 11 booster work exactly?
When Free 11 is active, all substitutions for that specific match cost 0 subs. You can change your entire squad and nothing is deducted. This is different from the Match 1/81 reset — Free 11 is a one-time booster you choose to use.
Can I use a booster in the playoffs?
No. Boosters are only available during the League Stage — Matches 2 to 80. The booster shelf is hidden completely during playoffs.
How are All Stars points calculated?
Your All Stars XI earns fantasy points every match using the same scoring system — with C/VC multipliers applied. No boosters apply. Points accumulate automatically across all 84 matches on a separate All Stars leaderboard.
What is the Strike Rate points system?
SR Points = Runs minus Balls Faced. If your batter scores 60 off 40 balls, they earn +20 SR points. If they score 10 off 25 balls, they lose −15 points. This rewards aggressive T20 batting heavily.
⚡ A hard-hitting innings can earn 20–30 extra points just from SR alone.
What is a private league?
Create a private league and share the invite code with friends. You compete on a separate leaderboard among just your group — while still playing on the global leaderboard too. Create or join from the Home page.
How do I see my match history?
Tap the History button on your Team View page. You'll see every match you've played, your score and subs used per match, with a full player breakdown for each game. Tap any player to see their individual point log.
