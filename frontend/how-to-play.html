Common Questions

When does the team lock?
Teams lock exactly at the "Actual Start Time" of the match to account for rain delays.
Why do I have negative subs?
This usually happens at a Phase Reset. Save your team during the "Reset Matches" (#41 or #53) to refresh your balance.
What is the Involvement Bonus?
Any player who records a non-zero fantasy score (batting, bowling, or fielding) receives an automatic +4 bonus.
