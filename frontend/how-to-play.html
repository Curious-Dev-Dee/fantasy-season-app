How to Play

Squad Building

Pick your 11-player squad with a 100 Credit budget:

  • WK: 1 Wicketkeeper
  • BAT: 3–6 Batters
  • AR: 1–4 All-Rounders
  • BOWL: 3–6 Bowlers
  • Overseas: Max 4

Transfer Strategy

Manage your subs carefully across the season:

League Stage (Matches 2–70) 130 Subs
Playoffs (Matches 72–74) 10 Subs

⚡ RESET WINDOWS: Matches #1 (Season Start) and #71 (Playoff Start) allow Unlimited Free Subs.

*1 free uncapped sub per match · Earn +1 free sub per 10 correct predictions in the Fun Zone!

What are Fun Zone Stars?
For every correct match prediction you earn 1 Star. Collect 10 Stars to automatically receive 1 Free Substitution added to your account!
How does "Free 11" work?
When you activate the Free 11 booster, any substitutions you make for that specific match cost 0 subs. Perfect for heavy rotation!
When do teams lock?
Teams lock exactly at the match start time. If the match is delayed, the timer updates automatically.
What is All Stars XI?
All Stars is a separate season-long team you build once before Match 1. It earns points every match but you can never change it after it locks. Check the Fun Zone tab!
What is Daily XI?
Daily XI lets you pick 11 players from only the two teams playing today. One entry per match, locked after saving. Compete on the Daily Leaderboard and track your Season Average Rank.
