Squad Building

Pick your 11 players squad using your 100 Credit budget. Your team must follow this tactical structure:

  • WK: 1 (Wicketkeeper)
  • BAT: 3 - 6 (Batsmen)
  • AR: 1 - 4 (All-Rounders)
  • BOWL: 3 - 6 (Bowlers)

When do teams lock?
Your team locks exactly at the "Actual Start Time" of the match. If the toss or match is delayed by rain, the lock time updates automatically.
What is the Involvement Bonus?
Any player who records a non-zero fantasy score (even just 1 run or 1 catch) earns a +4 bonus. This rewards players who are "in the game" even if they don't hit a big milestone.
How does the SR formula work?
We use a simple Runs - Balls formula. If a player scores 50 off 30 balls, they get +20 points. If they score 10 off 20 balls, they get -10 points. Volume matters!
Can I save subs for the Playoffs?
No. Your 150-sub bank is strictly for the League Stage. At Match #71, everyone is reset to a fresh 10-sub bank for the final sprint. Use 'em or lose 'em!
