How Analytics Work

📈 Total Points & Rank

Your total points are the sum of all your match scores across the season. Your rank is where you stand among all managers based on those points.

🏅 Tournament Percentile

"Top 12%" means you scored more than 88% of all managers. The smaller the number, the better you are doing.

🔥 Current Form (Dots)

Shows your last 5 matches newest first. Bright green dot = 250+ pts. Dim green = above your season average. Amber = near average. Red = below average. Quick way to see if you are hot or cold right now.

⚌ Consistency Score

Scores out of 100. Measures how similar your scores are match to match. Score 200 one match and 30 the next and you get a low score. Always scoring around the same number gives you a high score. 70+ is green, 50–69 is amber, below 50 is red.

📊 Score Trends (Last 3 / 6 / 10)

Your average score across your last 3, 6 or 10 matches. If your Last 3 average is higher than your season average you are in improving form. The bar chart below shows your score in every match with a dotted line for your season average.

→ Rank Journey

Shows how your rank changed across your last 10 matches. Each dot is one match. Going down on the chart means your rank improved — rank 1 is at the top. Gold dot means you were rank 1 after that match.

⇄ Subs Analysis

How many player transfers you have made. Shows your total subs used, your average per match, and your recent trend across last 3, 6 and 10 matches. Useful to see if you are rotating too much or staying too conservative.

🔥 Streaks

Best streak = how many matches in a row you scored 250 or more points. Worst streak = how many matches in a row you scored 150 pts or below. A best streak of 3 means you had 3 back-to-back big scoring matches.

👑 Captain Performance

In how many matches did your captain score above the average of all players in that match? 78% means your captain outperformed the field in 78 out of 100 matches. Good captaincy decisions directly lead to big scores.

🚀 Booster ROI

Shows every match where you used a booster and how many total points you scored that match. The best booster match is highlighted in gold. Helps you see which boosters gave the best returns and whether you timed them well.

⚔ Head to Head vs Rank 1

In how many individual matches did you score more points than the current rank 1 manager? Even if they beat you overall, you can still win individual matches. Shows how competitive you are against the best team in the tournament.

★ Top Point Earners

The 3 players who earned you the most fantasy points this season. Only counts matches where they actually played — if a player was in your team but scored 0 points they are not counted as having played.

❤ Most Loyal Players

The 3 players who actually took the field most often while in your team. Ranked by how many matches they played (points not equal to 0), not just how many times you picked them. A player picked 5 times but who only played 2 times will rank below one picked 4 times who played all 4.

👥 Player Usage

Unique Players Appeared = how many different players actually took the field while in your team this season. Total Appearances = the total count of all player appearances across all matches — Virat in 5 matches counts as 5. Avg Appearances per Match = total appearances divided by matches played. Avg Points per Appearance = your total season points divided by total appearances.

👥 Top by Role

Your best performing WK, BAT, AR and BOWL this season by total points earned while in your team. Tap each role tab to switch. Matches played shows how many times that player actually featured, not just how many times you picked them.

🏳 Player Categories

Breaks your squad into Indian, Overseas and Uncapped players. The colored bar shows what percentage of your total points came from each group. Tap each tab to see your top performers in that category. The Uncapped tab also shows a summary of how many times you used uncapped players and what they earned you on average.

🛡 Strong & Weak Zones

The app automatically analyses all your stats and picks up to 3 things you are doing really well (Strong Zone) and up to 3 things that are hurting your score (Weak Zone). Examples include elite captaincy, dropping form, underusing boosters, or being too dependent on overseas players.

⚖ Compare with Another Team

Pick any other manager to compare your stats side by side across 5 categories — Season, Captaincy, Subs, Boosters and Players. Green value = that team wins that stat. For subs and boosters used, fewer is better so the lower number is highlighted green. Good for settling arguments with your league friends.

🔁 Share Your Stats

Tap the Share button to share your season stats card with friends. On mobile it opens your native share menu. On desktop it copies the stats text to your clipboard so you can paste it anywhere.
