How Analytics Work

📊Total Points & Rank

Sum of all your match scores across the season. Rank = where you stand among all managers by points.

🏆Tournament Percentile

"Top 12%" means you scored more than 88% of all managers. Smaller = better.

🔥Current Form (Dots)

Your last 5 matches newest first. 🔥 = 15% above your avg. ✅ = above avg. 🟡 = near avg. ❌ = below avg.

⚙️Consistency Score

How similar are your scores match to match? Computed from score variance, captain hit rate, and sub efficiency. 70+ is elite, below 50 is volatile.

📈Score Trends

Your average over last 3, 6, 10 matches vs your season average. Green = improving, red = declining.

→Rank Journey

How your rank changed across your last 10 matches. Going down = rank improved. Gold dot = you were Rank 1.

⇄Subs Analysis

Total subs used, pace per match, and trend. Ideal pace is under 1.86/match across 70 matches.

🔥Streaks

Best streak = consecutive matches at 250+ pts. Worst streak = consecutive matches at 150 pts or below.

👑Captain Performance

% of matches where your captain scored above the average of all players that match. 70%+ = elite. League average is ~52%.

🚀Booster ROI

Every booster match and total score vs your no-booster average. Benchmark: TOTAL_2X avg 1,109 pts, INDIAN_2X 963, CAPTAIN_3X 811.

⚔️Head to Head vs Rank 1

How many individual matches did you score more points than today's Rank 1?

⭐Top Point Earners

Players who earned you the most points this season. Only counts matches where they actually played (points ≠ 0).

❤️Most Loyal Players

Players who took the field most often while in your team — ranked by actual appearances, not just selections.

🏴Player Categories

Points share from Indian, Overseas, and Uncapped players. Colored bar shows split. Tap tabs for top players in each group.

🛡️Strong & Weak Zones

Auto-generated from all your stats — up to 3 things you're doing well and 3 areas hurting your score.

⚖️Compare With Another Team

Side-by-side stat comparison across Season, Captaincy, Subs, Boosters, Players. Green = winner of that stat.
