How Analytics Work

📈 Total Points & Rank

Your total points are the sum of all your match scores across the season. Your rank is where you stand among all managers based on those points.

🏅 Tournament Percentile

"Top 12%" means you scored more than 88% of all managers. The smaller the number, the better you are doing.

🔥 Current Form (Dots)

Shows your last 5 matches newest first. Green dot = you scored 250+ pts or above your season average. Amber = near average. Red = below average. Quick way to see if you are hot or cold right now.

⚌ Consistency Score

Scores out of 100. Measures how similar your scores are match to match. If you always score around 90-100 you get a high score. If you score 200 one match and 30 the next you get a low score. 70+ is green, 50-69 is amber, below 50 is red.

📊 Score Trends (Last 3 / 6 / 10)

Your average score across just your last 3, 6 or 10 matches. Useful to see if you are improving recently. If Last 3 average is higher than your season average you are in good form.

→ Rank Journey

Shows how your rank changed across your last 10 matches. Each dot is one match. Going down on the chart means your rank improved (rank 1 is at the top). Gold dot means you were rank 1 in that match.

⇄ Subs Analysis

How many player transfers you have used. Total subs used across the season plus your recent average. Useful to see if you are making too many changes or being conservative.

🔥 Streaks

Best streak = how many matches in a row you scored 250 or more points. Worst streak = how many matches in a row you scored below 100 points. A best streak of 3 means you had 3 back-to-back big matches.

👑 Captain Performance

In how many matches did your captain score above the average of all players in that match? 78% means your captain outperformed the average player in 78 out of 100 matches. Good captaincy = big scores.

🚀 Booster ROI

Shows every match where you used a booster and how many total points you scored that match. Helps you see which boosters gave you the best returns and whether you timed them well.

⚔ Head to Head vs Rank 1

In how many individual matches did YOU score more points than the current overall rank 1 manager? Even if they beat you overall, you can still win individual matches. Shows how competitive you are against the best.

★ Top Point Earners

The 3 players who earned you the most fantasy points across all the matches they appeared in your team. Your most valuable players this season.

❤ Most Loyal Players

The players you picked most often across all matches. High matches in team means you kept them through the season instead of dropping them.

👥 Top by Role

Best performing WK, BAT, AR and BOWL in your team across the season by total points earned. Tap each role tab to switch between them.

⚖ Compare Mode

Pick any other manager and compare your key stats side by side. Green value = that team is winning that stat. Good for trash talk with your league friends.
