How Analytics Work

📈 Total Points & Rank

Your total points are the sum of all your match scores across the season. Your rank is where you stand among all managers based on those points.

🏅 Tournament Percentile

"Top 12%" means you scored more than 88% of all managers. The smaller the number, the better you are doing.

🔥 Current Form (Dots)

Shows your last 5 matches newest first. Bright green dot = 250+ pts. Dim green = above your season average. Amber = near average. Red = below average.

⚌ Consistency Score

Scores out of 100. Measures how similar your scores are match to match. 70+ is green, 50–69 is amber, below 50 is red.

📊 Score Trends (Last 3 / 6 / 10)

Your average score across your last 3, 6 or 10 matches. If your Last 3 average is higher than your season average you are in improving form.

→ Rank Journey

Shows how your rank changed across your last 10 matches. Going down on the chart means your rank improved — rank 1 is at the top. Gold dot means you were rank 1 after that match.

⇄ Subs Analysis

How many player transfers you have made. Shows your total subs used, your average per match, and your recent trend across last 3, 6 and 10 matches.

🔥 Streaks

Best streak = how many matches in a row you scored 250 or more points. Worst streak = how many matches in a row you scored 150 pts or below.

👑 Captain Performance

In how many matches did your captain score above the average of all players in that match? Good captaincy decisions directly lead to big scores.

🚀 Booster ROI

Shows every match where you used a booster and how many total points you scored that match. The best booster match is highlighted in gold.

⚔ Head to Head vs Rank 1

In how many individual matches did you score more points than the current rank 1 manager? Shows how competitive you are against the best team.

★ Top Point Earners

The 3 players who earned you the most fantasy points this season. Only counts matches where they actually played.

❤ Most Loyal Players

The 3 players who actually took the field most often while in your team. Ranked by how many matches they played, not just how many times you picked them.

👥 Player Usage

Unique Players Appeared = how many different players actually took the field. Total Appearances = total count across all matches. Avg Points per Appearance = total season points divided by total appearances.

👥 Top by Role

Your best performing WK, BAT, AR and BOWL this season by total points earned while in your team. Tap each role tab to switch.

🏳 Player Categories

Breaks your squad into Indian, Overseas and Uncapped players. The colored bar shows what percentage of your total points came from each group.

🛡 Strong & Weak Zones

The app automatically analyses all your stats and picks up to 3 things you are doing really well (Strong Zone) and up to 3 things that are hurting your score (Weak Zone).

⚖ Compare with Another Team

Pick any other manager to compare your stats side by side across 5 categories — Season, Captaincy, Subs, Boosters and Players. Green value = that team wins that stat.

🔁 Share Your Stats

Tap the Share button to share your season stats card with friends. On mobile it opens your native share menu. On desktop it copies the stats text to your clipboard.
